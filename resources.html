Our technical support team is available to assist with problems with downloads, error messages, audio files, suppleentary CDs, etc. Contact them at istech@byu.edu or 1-877-897-8085 (M-F from 8 a.m. - 6 p.m. MST)
Visit our support webpage for more information.

Tutoring Assistance

BYU Independent Study's tutoring program is free to all our students, and is available for most courses. Students are encouraged to take advantage of this service and recieve one-on-one assistance throughout their courses. Tutoring is by appointment on a first-come, first-served basis. Visit our tutors page or call us at 1-800-914-8931 to schedule an appointment.
