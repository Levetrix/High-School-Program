Standard Program

4-Year High School

Overview

Students meet program requirements by successfully completing 24 credit hours in specified subject areas. After successfully completing all program requirements, students recieve an official transcript and letter of completion.

The Standard and Advanced programs are for high-school-age students preparing for college or the job market. Upon completing these programs, students are issued an official transcript rather than a diploma. This official transcript can be used to applpy to colleges and universities or as proof of high school completion for potential employers.
