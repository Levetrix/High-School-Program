AP Course Info

BYU Independent Study offers AP Biology, AP Calculus, and AP Psychology courses. When a student completes an AP course and is ready to take the AP exam, they should do the following:

  • Contact the AP Office to find a testing location.

    Website: www.collegeboard.com

    Email: apexams@info.collegeboard.org

    Phone: 1-888-225-5427 (toll free) or 601-771-7300

  • Pay for the AP exam (the fee for the exam is not included in the cost of the course).
